About Our Project

Community Help Hub is a web-based platform designed to provide quick access to emergency services, healthcare information, government welfare schemes, educational resources, and community support services. The platform aims to improve public awareness and accessibility to essential information.

Andhra Pradesh Government Schemes

🎓 Thalliki vandanam

Eligibility: Students pursuing higher education.

Benefits: Reimbursement of tuition fees.

🏥 NTR Vydhyaseva

Eligibility: Eligible low-income families.

Benefits: Cashless medical treatment.

🌾Annadhatha Sukhibava

Eligibility: Farmers and cultivators.

Benefits: Financial support for agriculture.

👩 Aada Bidda Nidhi Scheme

Eligibility: Women beneficiaries aged 45-60 years.

Benefits: Financial assistance for livelihood support.

👵 NTR Bharosa Pension

Eligibility: Senior citizens and eligible beneficiaries.

Benefits: Monthly pension support.
